Cuppa Memory

How to Play

Welcome to Cuppa Memory!

This is a fun game that helps you memorize short passages by filling in missing words.

  • 1

    Enter a Passage

    Start by typing (or copy and pasting) a short passage (like a Bible verse) into the text box.

  • 2

    Start the Game

    Press the "Start Memorizing" button, and the game will begin!

  • 3

    Fill in the Blanks

    The passage will appear with one or more words missing. Type the correct missing word(s) in the input box.

  • 4

    Submit

    After entering your answer, press "Submit". If it's correct, you’ll get a “Correct!” message, and the button will change to "Next" for the next round.

  • 5

    Level Up

    As you progress, the game will remove more and more words from the passage, making it a bit harder. You’ll go through five rounds at each level, gradually filling in more blanks.

  • 6

    Final Round

    In the last two rounds, all the words will be missing! Type the entire passage to complete the game.

The goal is to memorize the passage by remembering the words and their order. Keep playing to improve your memory and have fun!
